About Outsider

Outsider is more than a name — it's a mindset. A wanderer, a lone rider crossing forgotten roads and abandoned cities. Once part of the system, now forging a path of his own — slow, deliberate, and free.

He believes in silence over noise, grit over glitter, and the kind of freedom only dust and fuel can buy.
