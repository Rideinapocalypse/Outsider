Welcome to Outsider

A digital space for the ones who walk off the map, who wander on motorbikes and dream with the stars. This is my first web page — built with curiosity and code.

About Outsider

Outsider is more than a name — it's a mindset. A wanderer, a lone rider crossing forgotten roads and abandoned cities. Once part of the system, now forging a path of his own — slow, deliberate, and free.

He believes in silence over noise, grit over glitter, and the kind of freedom only dust and fuel can buy.

Outsider's Gear

KTM 390 Adventure

Outsider’s trusted machine. Lightweight, fearless, and ready for chaos. Outfitted with Thoska soft panniers, it carries what matters — tools, survival gear, and a past he won’t talk about.

Airoh Commander Helmet

Dual-sport and apocalypse-ready. Built for dust, wind, rain, and silence. Outsider wears this when he disappears between places.

30L Rolltop Backpack

Waterproof. Lightweight. Quiet. Carries maps, notebooks, and spare fate. When the world ends, this is the bag you’ll want on your back.

Rusted Handgun

Not for show. Not for glory. This worn-out sidearm tells no stories — but it’s been in more than one. Outsider carries it not to fight, but to finish what others start.

Journey Log

DAY 12 – THE FACTORY

Left before sunrise. Smoke still rises from the east. The map said there was nothing here, but I heard machines through the wind.

DAY 41 – RADIO BLACKOUT

Signal’s gone. Even static is quiet now. I passed a sign that said “Welcome to Silence.” Makes sense.

DAY 88 – FROZEN ZONE

The cold isn’t just weather. It’s something else. Found an old road marker with my name carved in it. I didn’t put it there.
